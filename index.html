Gameplay
Dungeons and Dragons is a role-playing game. You play through a story told by the Game Master. The story and the game are driven by the decisions made by the players.

You will need a group of people to play with to get started. One of the people will play as the Game Master, who is the storyteller. You will also need a campaign, which is the story that you play. You will need 7 dice: the D20, D12, D10, D8, D6, and D4. The numbers are how many sides are on the dice. Campaigns can span from a couple of hours to entire days. Having a weekly playing session can help manage the time. Lastly, you will need to create your character using a character sheet.

Books like The Player's Handbook can help you get started with D&D. The book Dungeon Master's Guide explains how to make campaigns. The Monster Manual lists all of the different animals you might encounter in your campaign. While these resources aren't necessary, reading them can enhance your game play and I recommend that you get them.
